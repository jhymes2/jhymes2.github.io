Overview

Dartmouth Students' Robotics Team (DSRT) — Mechanical Lead and Treasurer since September 2024. Built four VEXU robots from concept to competition, optimizing each design for game-rule compliance and efficient assembly under the constraints of the collegiate VEXU program.

Implemented an inventory management system to track parts and purchase requests across the team. Optimized 3D printing workflows for common components, reducing hardware access friction, cutting costs, and eliminating build bottlenecks during crunch periods. Also implemented path tracking and pose estimation software for autonomous operation.

Process

Each season begins with game analysis — breaking down scoring elements and field constraints to identify the highest-leverage strategies. Robots are designed around those strategies in Onshape, then manufactured and programmed for competition. Alongside the build cycle, onboarding and teaching new members is a core part of the role, growing team capacity each year.
