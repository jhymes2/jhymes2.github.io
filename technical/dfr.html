Low Voltage Electrical Team

Type Circuit Design, PCB Assembly, Electrical Validation
Status Ongoing
Year 2024 – Present
Tools KiCad, SMD Soldering, Lab Equipment

Overview

Dartmouth Formula Racing (DFR) — Low Voltage Electrical Team member since October 2024. Working on the electrical systems of DFR's Formula SAE car, with a focus on battery monitoring and sensor integration at the circuit level.

Designed multiplexer circuitry to add temperature probe channels, expanding the car's battery monitoring capacity. Assembled PCBs using SMD soldering techniques and performed electrical validation with lab equipment to troubleshoot integration issues across the low voltage system.

Process

Work begins at the circuit level — identifying where existing monitoring capacity falls short, then designing solutions in KiCad. Boards are assembled in-house and validated against the car's electrical requirements before integration, with oscilloscopes and multimeters used throughout to diagnose any issues that arise during testing.
