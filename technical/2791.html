Overview

Shaker Robotics, FRC Team 2791 — Team Manager and Mechanical Lead from October 2021 through June 2024. Over three build seasons, led the design and assembly of three 125-lb competition robots, earning 7 top-3 regional finishes and qualifying for the World Championship.

Managed a team of 100+ members across all subteams, implementing schedules and workflows that improved overall build season efficiency by 30%. Built and maintained a purchase order system tracking $100K+ in annual expenses and overhauled hardware organization across the shop.

Beyond competition, founded community outreach programs that delivered 90+ robotics workshops to 80+ students — growing the pipeline of young engineers into the program.

Build Seasons

Each six-week build season began with game analysis and requirements definition, followed by rapid mechanical prototyping, CAD in Inventor, machining, and full assembly. Subsystem design, weight budgeting, and drive train selection were recurring mechanical challenges, with competition iterations driven by match data and strategic alliance feedback.
