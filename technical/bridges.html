Bridges

Type Mechanical Engineering
Status Completed
Years 2023 & 2025
Tools SolidWorks, Illustrator, Laser Engraver

Capital Region Bridge Competition — 2023

Competed in the Capital Region Bridge Building Competition, designing, building, and assembling a bridge optimized to hold maximum weight at minimum mass. Structural analysis was performed in ModelSmart. The bridge set a new student record by holding 968 lbs — placing first in weight held, first in weight-to-mass efficiency, and third in bridge aesthetics.

Dartmouth Solid Mechanics Bridge — 2025

Designed and fabricated a plywood bridge for Dartmouth's Solid Mechanics course. Produced hand truss force calculations and used SolidWorks FEA to predict deflection and maximum load. Laser cut and assembled the bridge with controlled joints.
