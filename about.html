About

Jonah Hymes

Engineering & Fine Art · Dartmouth College

Innovative engineering student with experience in robotics, entrepreneurship, and sustainable technology. Passionate about leveraging technical skills to design impactful solutions, especially in the field of environmental conservation.

Raised in upstate New York, I've always been around nature and on trails. I hold an affinity for the outdoors and a strong passion to have a positive impact on the natural environments I grew up around.

I love listening to music — current favorites are Fleetwood Mac and Mac DeMarco. I also love reading; favorite authors are Annie Dillard and Mary Oliver, particularly Pilgrim at Tinker Creek and Upstream.
